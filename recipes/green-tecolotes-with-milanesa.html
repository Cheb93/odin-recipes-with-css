Green Tecolotes with Milanesa

Ingredients

  • Bolillo
  • Refried Beans
  • Cheese
  • Green Chilaquiles
  • Classic Milanesa

Cooking steps

  1. Cut the bolillo in half like making molletes.
  2. Spread 2 spoons of refried beans on both inner sections of the bolillo.
  3. Cut the milanesa in squares or stripes
  4. Set the oven to 200 °C.
  5. Put 2 or 3 floors of green chilaquiles over the beans.
  6. Allocate the milanesa's pieces over the chilaquiles.
  7. Cover all with cheese.
  8. Put it in the oven for 15 minutes.
  9. Serve.
